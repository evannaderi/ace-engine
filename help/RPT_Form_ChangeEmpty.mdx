## Who does this affect?

* Blind people using screen readers
* Many older adults
* People with learning disabilities

## Why is this important?

Forms that are designed to automatically submit when all the fields are filled out or when focus leaves the last field without providing a warning can be confusing to users.

## Confirm the form does not submit automatically
Do not automatically submit a form without warning the user

[IBM 3.2.2 On Input](http://www.ibm.com/able/guidelines/ci162/on_input.html) | [WCAG 2.1 technique G13](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/Techniques/general/G13) | [WCAG 2.1 failure technique F36](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/Techniques/failures/F36)

<div id="elementLocation">
</div>

## What to do

* Use the standard behavior of a `<form>` element by providing a "submit" button or the use of an Enter key to submit the form.
